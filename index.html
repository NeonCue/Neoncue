About Us

We offer a codec-agnostic solution that optimizes video compression rates,
enabling businesses to reduce costs while ensuring a premium content experience
for customers with minimal bandwidth usage

Demo

Problem

SURGE IN ONLINE VIDEO CONSUMPTION

The growing demand for online video content has led to network congestion issues

CUSTOMER SATISFACTION

Users often experience degraded video quality, resulting in pixelation and buffering, which leads to user frustration and dissatisfaction

HIGH STORAGE COSTS

Service providers are grappling with the ever-increasing volume of video data. They are burdened by rising expenses for cloud storage or substantial investments in storage infrastructure.

Solution

AI-BASED VIDEO PROCESSING

We employ our proprietary AI technology to process video signals before and after conventional video codecs for better compression

OPTIMIZE VIDEO COMPRESSION

The content- and context- aware solution maximizes video compression rates, significantly reducing size up to 50% while maintaining the video quality

CODEC-AGNOSTIC

The solution works with conventional video codecs in an interoperable manner. This approach enables our solution can be adopted in broad services




NeonCue software achieves a 47% BD-rate saving over the HEVC codec.
