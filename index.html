About NeonCue

The global consumption of digital video is increasing at an astronomical rate leading to increased storage costs and transmission issues. We are passionate about pioneering new AI technology that will reduce the size of files drastically and save companies billions of dollars in storage costs.

The Problem




DATA USAGE IS SKYROCKETING




CLOUD STORAGE COSTS ARE INCREASING 300% A YEAR





CLOGGED DATA PIPES CREATE TRANSMISSION ISSUES

Solution




AI-BASED VIDEO PROCESSING

We employ our proprietary AI technology to process video signals before and after conventional video codecs for better compression




INCREASED QUALITY AT A LOWER FILE SIZE

The content- and context- aware solution maximizes video compression rates, significantly reducing size up to 50% while maintaining the video quality




SEAMLESS INTEGRATION WITH YOUR WORKFLOW

Our products work with conventional video codecs in an interoperable manner, allowing it to work across a variety of services
